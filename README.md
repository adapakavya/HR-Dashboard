### 5. Features and Highlights

### Business Problem

Healthcare teams often lack an easy way to interpret survival trends and risk factors from raw clinical data. Identifying which age groups or health conditions contribute to higher mortality is critical.

### Goal of the Dashboard

‚Ä¢ To provide a visual analytical tool that highlights:<br>
‚Ä¢ Survival trends across age categories<br>
‚Ä¢ Influence of serum sodium levels on survival<br>
‚Ä¢ Impact of diabetes on patient outcomes<br>
‚Ä¢ Gender-based differences in survival<br>
